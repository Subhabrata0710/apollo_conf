About the Conclave

Leading Pediatric Healthcare Forward

A Premier International Pediatric Congress

We are delighted to announce the ABC Children’s Conclave , a prestigious international academic event organized by Apollo Children's, Apollo Multispecialty Hospital Kolkata, in association with West Bengal Academy of Paediatrics in collaboration with Boston Children's Hospital, USA.

The conclave is scheduled to be held on 8th–10th May 2026 at Hyatt Regency, Kolkata. This landmark event is expected to host over 400 practicing pediatricians, postgraduate trainees, and leading faculty from across India and abroad, including distinguished experts from Boston Children's Hospital.

The scientific program will feature subspeciality symposia, panel discussions, interactive sessions, and global perspectives on contemporary pediatric practice.
