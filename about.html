Conference Highlights

Scientific Sessions

Comprehensive presentations covering the full spectrum of pediatric specialties, including cardiology, neurology, oncology, gastroenterology, neonatology, and emergency medicine. Each session is designed to provide actionable clinical insights.

Panel Discussions

Thought-provoking dialogues addressing critical challenges in pediatric healthcare, including emerging infectious diseases, mental health, healthcare delivery systems, and ethical considerations.

Research Presentations

Platform for showcasing groundbreaking research, innovative case studies, and clinical trials. Opportunities for abstract presentations and poster exhibitions by young researchers and fellows.
